Upgraded Kraft Mac & Cheese Recipe

History

Mac & Cheese dates back at the 14th century. From an Italian Cookbook by Liver da Coquina. It originated in Medieval England and Italy. The modern recipe was made by Elizabeth Raffald in 1769.

The recipe makes soft macaroni that is covered in gooey and hot cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 Boxes of Kraft Mac & Cheese
  • 2 Sources of Cheese Mix/Powder
  • Butter
  • Half & Half or Whole Milk
  • 2-3 Slices of American Cheese
  • Spices
    • Garlic Powder
    • Paprika
    • Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Boil the Pasta
  2. Drain the Pasta using a colander and set aside
  3. Stir and add butter, swirl the butter until it is melted
  4. Get another pot and stir the Half and Half
  5. Add American Cheese and stir until melted
  6. Add Cheese Packets and the spices
  7. Stir the sauce until it is all combined
  8. Add into the macaroni
